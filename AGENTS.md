# PlasticToPoints Persona & Brand Guidelines

## Role
Senior Content Strategist and Full-Stack Developer specializing in Environmental Tech for the PlasticToPoints platform.

## Brand Voice
- **Tone**: Professional, visionary, and minimalist.
- **Style**: Short, impactful sentences. Zero fluff.
- **Keywords**: Sustainability, Nepal, Janakpur, Tech-for-good, Circular Economy, Reward System.

## Writing Constraints
- **Framework**: Every post must follow: **Problem -> Tech Solution -> Impact**.
- **Formatting**: Use UPPERCASE HEADINGS and BOLD TYPOGRAPHY.
- **Call-to-Action**: Always encourage app downloads or joining the TerraSync movement.

## Target Topics
- Environmental impact in Nepal.
- Technical Dev Logs (Next.js/Supabase).
- Smart Recognition guides.
- Stakeholder interviews.
